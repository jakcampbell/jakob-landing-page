# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Personal Landing Page – Jakob Campbell

---

## 1. Problem Statement

As a Business Analytics student seeking internships, I need a centralized and professional online presence that clearly showcases my skills, experience, and projects. Currently, my resume alone does not fully demonstrate my technical abilities, business experience, or personal brand.

---

## 2. Target Audience

- Recruiters for analytics and business internships  
- Hiring managers in data analytics, business analytics, and related roles  
- Professors or professionals reviewing my work  
- Potential clients or connections interested in my experience  

---

## 3. Goals & Success Metrics

### Goals:
- Clearly communicate my background and skills  
- Highlight my business experience (Plainfield PowerWash)  
- Showcase technical and analytical capabilities  
- Create a strong first impression for recruiters  

### Success Metrics:
- Visitors can understand who I am within 10 seconds  
- Clear navigation between sections  
- Increased chances of interview callbacks  
- Professional and visually clean design  

---

## 4. Required Features / Content

### Header Section
- Name: Jakob Campbell  
- Headline: Business Analytics & Information Systems Student  
- Quick intro  

### About Section
- Short summary of background  
- Focus on analytics + business + communication  

### Experience Section
- Plainfield PowerWash (Founder)
- Brand Ambassador (Eventlink)
- Jewel-Osco (Cashier)

### Skills Section
- Python, SQL, Excel  
- Tools (GitHub, VS Code, APEX, etc.)

### Contact Section
- Email  
- LinkedIn  
- GitHub  

---

## 5. Scope

### In Scope:
- Single-page landing website  
- Responsive design  
- Clean, modern layout  
- Basic styling using HTML/CSS  

### Out of Scope:
- Backend functionality  
- Login systems  
- Complex animations  

---

## 6. Assumptions & Constraints

### Assumptions:
- Users will access via desktop and mobile  
- Recruiters will spend limited time on page  
- Content should be quick and easy to scan  

### Constraints:
- Built using basic HTML and CSS  
- Hosted on Azure Static Web Apps  
- Limited development time
